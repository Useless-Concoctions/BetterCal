# Roadmap

BetterCal is a premium, localized social calendar and personal schedule designed to bridge the gap between static scheduling, social events, and the fluid reality of daily life. This roadmap outlines our path from a utility tool to a comprehensive intelligence layer.

- **[Vision](docs/VISION.md)**: Product vision statement and long-term north-star goals.

---

## The Product

Immediate focus on expanding active workflow tools and delivering a high-fidelity mobile experience.

- [ ] **Mobile Native**: Bringing the premium drag-and-drop experience and liquid scheduling to an offline-first iOS/Android application.
- [ ] **Collaboration**: Shared ghost goals and social calendar curation for teams, families, and friend groups.
- [ ] **Insights**: AI-driven weekly reports on time allocation, goal completion rates, and focus continuity.

---

## SoCal Features

Initiatives to transition SoCal into a high-fidelity, autonomous event discovery experience by closing the "distance" between user interests and fragmented community pulses.

- [x] **Hub UI**: High-fidelity dashboard for exploring subscribable calendar collections.
- [ ] **Temporal Subscriptions**: State and sync logic for persistent public-to-personal calendar subscriptions.
- [ ] **Frictionless Ingestion**: High-resolution Gemini parser for flyers, Reddit threads, and Luma pages.
- [ ] **Channel Branding**: Verified profiles and public sub-calendar ownership for local curators.
- [ ] **Collective Pulse**: Aggregate interest visualization without compromising individual privacy.
