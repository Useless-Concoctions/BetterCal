# Product Vision

SoCal aims to eliminate the "manual labor" of event discovery by transitioning the calendar into a **Followable Feed of Time**, closing the distance between latent personal interests and the fragmented reality of local community pulses.

## The North Star

- **Temporal Subscriptions**: High-fidelity event streams that replace the manual labor of checking fragmented sources like Reddit, Luma, and Eventbrite.
- **Frictionless Socialization**: High-resolution AI ingestion that converts snapshots, flyers, and messy group coordination into structured, public event streams.
- **Collective Consciousness**: A lived experience where the personal schedule reflects the real-time presence and pulse of the communities you follow.
